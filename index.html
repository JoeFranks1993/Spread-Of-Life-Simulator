Spread Of Life Simulation

This is my attempt at duplicating an experiment done in Steven Hawking's "The Meaning Of life.

Everything works as I intended it however after completing this i found that JavaScript is only given one thread to use on the processor. This is why i changed to checking each square randomly as opposed to checking them all at the same time and changing them. A major difference but the programmatic point is met.

Here is the original experiment

When called upon, each square checks its neighbor’s state. A green square is alive, a grey square is dead. If a living square has 3 or more live neighbors it dies of over population. If a dead square is surrounded by more than 2 live neighbor’s life spreads to it and it becomes alive. if a living square has no living neighbors it dies of under population.
